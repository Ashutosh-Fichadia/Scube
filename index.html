Specifications

Base & Outside Walls

Base frame used are of MS steel channel 100 x 50 mm, 75 x 40 mm with cross members of 60 x 40 x 2 mm pipe.

Outside walls from all MS 1.2 mm vertically corrugated MS sheet panels continuously welded to top side and base structure for better strength and weight. All gaps sealed at edges and at seams, bottom etc for preventing ingress of moisture.

Inside framing of MS L and specially design C for give better strength to cladding. Specially deign Window frames to avoid Rain water penetration. 6” weather shed on all door and windows.
